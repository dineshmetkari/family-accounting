Bank Account Customizing

A bank account is a financial account recording the financial transactions between the customer and their bank and the resulting financial position of the customer with the bank.

Fields

1. Bank account identity(char[10])
 The identity format is <bank key>_<account last four number>.  For example, identity "CMB_6620" is bank account **********6620 in bank key CMB.
2. Bank account description(String)
3. Bank key
4. Bank account type
5. Bank account number(char[16])
