G/L Account Customizing

The General Ledger Accouting(G/L Account) is same with the account in Double-entry bookkeeping system.

Fields,

1. G/L account identity(char[10])
 All characters in G/L account identity is numberic.
2. G/L account description(String)
3. G/L account type
4. Bank account
5. G/L account group.
